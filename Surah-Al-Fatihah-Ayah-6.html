Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 6

The path of those whom you favored

The path that is made smooth and luxurious

Like a path going up an escalator

These people made effort and Allah made the path easy for them

These people are only found in the past

You take advice from those who already tread this path

Those who Allah has guided in the past

The Quran is full of stories of people of the past

Every story will help you on this path

Their stories are my companions on this journey

These people are the Prophets

Those who confirmed truth in the Prophets

The martyrs

And the righteous

Those who had beneficial knowledge and acted upon it
