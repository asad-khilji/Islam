Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 3

The first three ayat of Surah Al Fatihah introduce us to Allah

Praise and Thanks belongs to Allah because He is Rabb, Ar Rahman, Ar Raheem, and Malik Yom Ad Deen

When someone is extremely loving and caring, people take adavantage of their love and care

Some people take it as an open license to do whatever they want

Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem is two most beautiful qualities of Allah

But it can create an imbalance in people and people can take advantage of the love and care of Allah

There is a story of a Master and a slave

The Master tells the slave do whatever you want but do not cross the fence

One day the slave goes close to the line and trips on the other side of the fence

The master sees and says nothing

The next day the slave pretends to fall and master says nothing

Now the slave has one foot on one side of the fence and the other foot on the other side of the fence

The master says nothing

The slave spends all his time on the other side of the fence

Then one day the master calls the slave over

The master says to the slave remeber I told you not to cross the fence

The slave says yes

The master says I recorded all the times you crossed the fence and decided to punish you now all at once

We call Allah our Master and He told us not to cross certain lines

When some of us slip into sin we immediately repent and turn back to Allah

Then we realize nothing happened and that Allah is extremely loving and caring so we fall more into sin

When people remind us not to sin we often say Allah is Ar Rahman Ar Raheem

Allah is extremely loving and caring

Rabb includes the name Master and gift giver

Malik only includes the name Master

Rahma or love and care of Allah is being removed from the conversation now

Malik means owner and king

A owner owns small things and a king owns big things like a kingdom

A king cannot control every small detail

Allah is the king and an owner at the same time

Allah has full control on the day of judgment both big and small

On the day of judgment big things will happen mountains will erupt and oceans will boil

This requires the authority of a king

Every small deed of ours is also counted on the day of judgment

Day is a unit of time

Human beings have expierence of ownership but we will never own time

Allah takes ownership of time which only He can take ownership of

Allah can make this day 50,000 years because he owns and controls it

Time is like a bag and we are inside this bag

When Allah owns the day He owns everything inside that day

Everything Allah created exists inside of time

Ad Deen means Judgment

How you deal with someone and how you will be dealt with

It is the completing of the record which is why we say Alhamdulillah

Humans beings desire justice expecially for other people

This world is unfair and a day will come where justice will be served

No crime will go uncompensated

Think of a number line you have positive, neutral, and negative

The Love and Care of Allah is positive

The Justice of Allah is neutral

The Punishment of Allah is negative

Allah does not compare His Love and Care with His punishment

But rather He compares His Love and Care with His Justice

It is the Justice of Allah that drives people into the hell fire

It is the Love and Care of Allah that drives people into Jannah

There is one scene on Judgment

You are walking past ready with your book in your right hand in front of you turned towards laytul qadr

The angels say its ok you can go into Jannah

And you enter Jannah without being questioned

Some people have this privalege of an easy auditing on Judgment day

Human Being what deluded you and turned you away from your Generous Rabb

Allah is saying I was extremely loving and caring with you

Allah gave us so many chances and we took advantage of those chances

You remembered Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem but forgot about Malik Yom Ad Deem

How could you do that to yourself

In these first three ayat of the Fatihah is the complete introduction to Allah

Here Allah is talking about Himself

In the next few ayat in the Fatihah we are talking to Allah

It is a direct conversation between us and Allah

Fatihah is teaching us we have to engage in a conversation with Allah

First Allah is talking to us and now we are talking to Allah

No one is between us and Allah, we have a direct connection with Allah

Messengers came to connect the people with Allah

Every person is being honored in the Fatihah because Allah the Most High is talking to you and me directly

Important people talk to the crowd but the crowd do not get to talk to important people

Allah addresses all of humanity and now we are engaging in conversation with Allah
