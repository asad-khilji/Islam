Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 4

It is only to You we give ourselves in slavery and worship

We make decisons based on our thinking and what our hearts our compelled towards

We thought about who Allah is and our emotions were shaken as well as our minds

Everytime we recite this surah we are starting again with our relationship with Allah

We must revisit why we are Muslim?

This is surah is screaming to us to refresh our commitment to Allah

We made a commitment to Allah not to cross the line throughout my life

It is a very difficult commitment and we need help

So the next thing we ask for is Allah's help

We seek your help and only Your help after exhausting ourselves in effort

We want to be Allah's slave after knowing He is Rabb, Ar Rahman, Ar Raheem, and Malik Yom Deen

Now that we are the slaves of Allah we are now worthy of Allah's help

Allah mentioned slavery first then help second

We fulfilled our purpose by being a slave first

First you do the job then you get the benefits

Allah's help is the added benefit for you

One part of this ayah is for Allah and the other part is for us

The help of Allah is granted after exhausting yourself in effort

There are two scenarios

In the first scenario you have a flat tire

Then you ask for help and you sit in the driving seat as he is changing the tire

In the second scenario you ask for help after exhausting yourself in effort

There are two extremes

One extreme is Allah does everything and I do nothing

The other extreme is I did everything

Both these extremes are wrong and we have a middle path in our Deen

We must put in the effort first

If we do not put the effort in we will not qualify for Allah's help

The efforts are in our hands and the results are in the hands of Allah

If we do not show effort then the results from Allah will not come

When you put in the effort then the results from Allah will come on Allah's terms

We asked for help but we did not specify what we need help in

It is because we are so desperate that we are short on words

For example if you are hanging from a tree about to fall from a cliff you do not say...

I am falling from this cliff about to fall from such a high altitude

Instead we just scream HELP!!!

We also do not specify what we need help in because we need help in everything and the list is too long

These ayat are about someone finally coming to Islam and finding Allah then desperately asking Allah for help
