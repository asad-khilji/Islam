Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 7

But not the path of those of those who had wrath directed upon them

Nor of those who are lost

In ayah 6 a verb is used because those who Allah has favored are only found in the past

This ayah is noun based which means those who had anger upon them are a timeless problem

You will find these people in the past present and future

Those who received anger are those who had beneficial knowledge but did not act upon it

Those who are lost are those who had no knowledge but acted wrongly

Those who had wrath upon them are the Jews mentioned in the Quran but not all the Jews

Those who are lost are the Christians mentioned in the Quran but not all the Christians

There is an example of Imad and Waleed

I go upstairs for a phone call and the two boys are looking at the cookies

Before I go I tell Imad not to eat the cookies

Waleed can't eat them either but I leave Imad in charge and tell him not to eat

When I come back I see Imad and Waleed eating the cookies

I am angry at Imad because he knew not to eat the cookies but still did so

I am angry at Waleed but not as much as Imad

Waleed did not know but he should of asked me not Imad

This is the analogy between those who had wrath upon them and those who are lost
