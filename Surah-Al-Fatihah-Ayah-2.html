Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 2

Rahma comes from the same root word as the womb of the mother

Rahm is the womb of the mother

In this state you do not have to worry about anything

Everything is taken care of for you

The child is comletely wrapped up in the care of the mother

The child has no idea what Allah does to take care of you

The Rahma of Allah is similiar in this way

The heart is pumping blood constantly and we are unaware

We are not even remembering Allah and He is still taking care of us

We are just as ignorant as a child in the womb of the mother

Allah does all this out of unconditional love and care for us

Allah is taking care of us all the time constantly

The mother goes out of the way to take care of us

There are three qualities of the word Ar Rahman

The first quality is that Allah is extremely loving and caring

The second quality of Ar Rahman is that Allah's extreme love and care for us is immediate

The third quality of Ar Rahman is that the extreme love and care of Allah is temporary

There are two qualities of the word Ar Raheem

The first quality is that Allah is always extreme loving and caring

The second quality is that Allah's extreme love and care for us is not happening right now

For example your wife is making dinner

While making dinner she says what do you want to eat tomorrow

You say I do not care about tomorrow I need to eat right now

Then after you eat you say what about tomorrow we can have this or that

We need our immediate needs taken care of first then our future needs

Allah mentions His name Ar Rahman first then Ar Raheem

Allah takes care of our immediate needs first then he takes care of our future

Allah knows who He created that we are impatient human beings

Ibn Abbas said Ar Rahman is for everyone in this world

Ar Raheem is for believers in the next life

You cannot imagine an ocean with high waves and calm waves both at the same time

The qualities of Ar Rahman is like an ocean with high waves

The qualities of Ar Raheem is like an ocean with calm waves

Allah is both qualities at the same time

The love and care of Allah is beyond human comprehension

Surah Al Fatihah begins with Praise and Thanks is for Allah

Then we call Allah our Master

And the quality of that Master is being extremely loving and caring

Now tell me what other Master other than Allah is associated with this quality

There is no one else this much loving and caring

You cannot even be loving and caring to your own self

Whatever Allah tells us is because He loves us

Whatever Allah tells us to do it benefits us only

Allah does not benefit from it and does not need anything
