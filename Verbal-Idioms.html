Verbal Idioms in the Quran

In language there are different ways of saying something

For instance we have…

I give and I give up

I give means to give something like a gift

I give up means to quit

There is also sight and site

Sight means to see

Site means a construction site for a construction worker

Site could also mean a website site for a web developer

A Journal could mean different things to different people

An accounting journal

An academic journal

A personal journal

Language is alive and dynamic and it has different figures of speech

For instance Its cool, its popping, its cracking meaning its awesome

Its cool is also talking about the weather

Words are not meant literally

What’s up meaning what is above you

Whats up meaning how are you

Allah uses figures of speeches in the Quran

Coolness of the Eyes meaning shedding tears of joy

Coolness of the eyes also means Finding relief from a storm

Coolness of the eyes also means When something stays in one place

Warmness of the eyes used to curse someone

May your eyes become warm meaning may you suffer sadness

May your eyes become warm meaning may you shed tears of sadness

For example there are two mothers at the airport

One mother is picking up her son and the other mother is dropping her son

The mother picking up her son is expierencing coolness of the eyes

The mother dropping her son is expierencing the warmness of the eyes

When Arabs would travel in the desert they would wear heavy clothes wrapping themselves

But he cannot cover his eyes because he needs to see

Then there is a sand storm and his eyes are warm he cant see

Then he finds a cave and he can open his eyes and his eyes are cool

An assassin once said the eyes of my people will stay warm and the blood of my sword will cool them

There is a dua in the Quran which says O Allah give us from our Spouses and children the coolness of our eyes

Our spouses and children are a refuge from the storm

The outside world is a storm and the house is a relief from the storm

When you come back home to your spouses and children you find relief

And your eyes are cooled

We feel safe and the storm cannot hit us anymore

We want a happy house

We cry tears of joy looking at our spouses and children

In Surah Qasas the mother of Musa threw Musa in the river then her eyes became warm

The mother of musa is reunited with Musa and her eyes become cool

The wife of Firawn expierences the warmness of the eyes with Firawn

The wife of Firawn expierences the coolness of the eyes with baby Musa

Musa finds the warmness of the eyes while talking with his nation

Musa finds the coolness of the eyes while talking with Allah

Prophet Muhammad finds the warmness of his eyes when talking with his people

The Prophet Muhammad finds the coolness of his eyes when talking to Allah in salah

Allah says to lower your wings of humility out of love and care for your parents

A man does not have wings

It means to be humble

A person is being compared to a bird

When a bird raises its wings it is about to fly

When a bird lowers its wings it is about to land

Lower your wings means come back down to earth

When the bird has wings it lowers them

A child lowers his or her wings of humility before their parents

Our parents did everything for us

Children need to be humble before their parents when we grow up

When we grow up we have everything but we must lower our wings to our parents because of the love and care they showed children

Children must genuinely show love and care to parents

Children sacrificing for the parents like parents did for children

The roles are reversed

Musa makes a dua to Allah to untie the tongue from my tongue

a knot is in a string

Like in wires and threads

When you have a Knot that is jumbled up you do not know where it begins or where it ends

It is unclear and confusing

Untie the knot of my tongue means to give clarity in speech

To not get nervous when speaking with people

A clear beginning, a clear middle, and a clear end

To remain calm and not get angry or frustrated
